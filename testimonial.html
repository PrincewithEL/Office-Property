Welcome to Office Rental Management

When property search in Kenya used to be manual, one had to spend his/her valuable time taking trips to their local county recorder, clerk, or accessors office to inquire about any available property on sale. Property to let was also advertised in magazines and newspapers or posters or through mass media. Some of these platforms were conniving and were only meant to steal from interested tenants or leasers.

Finding rental office space on some of the available platforms is difficult and as a result, thus there is a need for a Web Based Management System which will be able to centralize the process of property search with all information in a single website and legal documents being recorded and saved in a centralized system. This is our mission and purpose.
